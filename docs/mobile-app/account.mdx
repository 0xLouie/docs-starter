---

Your Linkr account is your identity on the network. It connects your contributions, connections, and preferences across all devices.

---

## Signing in

When you open the app:

1. Tap **Sign In**
2. Enter your email address
3. Enter your password
4. Tap **Continue**

If your credentials are correct, you are signed in and taken to the map view.

---

## Creating an account

If you do not have a Linkr account:

1. Tap **Create Account** on the welcome screen
2. Enter your email address
3. Create a password
4. Confirm your password
5. Tap **Create Account**
6. Check your email for a verification link
7. Tap the link to verify your account

Once verified, you can sign in and start using the app.

---

## Staying signed in

The app keeps you signed in automatically. You do not need to enter your credentials each time you open the app.

To sign out manually:

1. Go to **Account**
2. Scroll to the bottom
3. Tap **Sign Out**

You might sign out if you are switching accounts or handing your device to someone else.

---

## Password recovery

If you forget your password:

1. On the sign-in screen, tap **Forgot Password**
2. Enter your email address
3. Tap **Send Reset Link**
4. Check your email for the reset link
5. Follow the link to create a new password
6. Return to the app and sign in with your new password

Password reset links expire after 24 hours.

---

## Account settings

In the **Account** section of the app, you can:

- **Update your email**: change the email associated with your account
- **Change your password**: set a new password
- **Manage notifications**: control what alerts you receive
- **View account activity**: see recent sign-ins and actions
- **Delete your account**: permanently remove your account and data

---

## Multiple devices

Your Linkr account works across devices. Sign in on a new device, and your data syncs automatically:

- Hotspots you have shared appear in your dashboard
- Your connection history is available
- Your preferences carry over

There is no limit to the number of devices you can sign in on.

---

## Account security

To keep your account secure:

- Use a strong, unique password
- Do not share your credentials
- Sign out on shared or public devices
- Review account activity periodically

If you notice suspicious activity, change your password immediately and contact support.

<Note>
Your Linkr credentials are the same whether you use the mobile app or the web. One account, everywhere.
</Note>

# Linkr Mobile App

The Linkr mobile app is the primary interface for discovering, connecting to, and sharing hotspots. It puts the entire Linkr network in your pocket.

<Note>
The Linkr app will be available on the App Store soon. Join the waitlist at linkrmap.com to be notified when it launches.
</Note>

---

## What you can do

The app provides full access to the Linkr network:

- **Discover hotspots**: browse the live map to find connectivity anywhere in the world
- **Connect to hotspots**: access available hotspots with a single tap
- **Share your own hotspots**: contribute connectivity and see it appear on the global map
- **Monitor your contributions**: track availability, reliability, and usage of hotspots you share
- **Manage your account**: update settings, view history, and control your presence on the network

Everything you can do on Linkr, you can do from the app.

---

## Requirements

- iOS 15.0 or later
- iPhone or iPad
- Internet connection for initial setup and map browsing
- Location services (optional but recommended for proximity-based discovery)

---

## Getting the app

When the app launches:

1. Search for "Linkr" on the App Store
2. Download and install
3. Open the app and sign in with your Linkr account (or create one)
4. Start exploring

If you do not have a Linkr account yet, you can create one directly in the app during first launch.

---

## App sections

The app is organized into four main areas:

**Map**: the live connectivity map showing all shared hotspots

**My Hotspots**: hotspots you have shared, with status and performance data

**Connections**: your connection history and any active sessions

**Account**: profile settings, preferences, and account management
